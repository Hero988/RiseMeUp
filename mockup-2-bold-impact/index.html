Who We Are

RiseMeUp Sustainable Farming is a non-profit charity dedicated to supporting the Nuba Mountain community in Sudan — a resilient people who have survived genocide and continue to face immense challenges.

Based in London, we are committed to growing rural communities through sustainable development, transforming 130 acres of poor-land into thriving horticulture, farming operations, and green energy infrastructure.

Our Purpose

Where We're Headed

Our Vision

Taking steps towards sustainable development by developing 130 acres of poor-land into horticulture, farming and implementing Green Energy as the main source of infrastructure for the farm. We envision a self-sustaining community powered by renewable energy and agricultural innovation.

VISION

Our Mission

Turning 130 acres of non-productive poor-land into a productive farm land, to create new agricultural job opportunities to help, support the neediest and most excluded within this rural community to end poverty. We believe in empowering people through sustainable agriculture.

MISSION
The 4 Phases

Our Projects

A comprehensive four-phase approach to transform the Nuba Mountain region through sustainable farming, green energy, and modern agricultural technology.

Phase 01

Biogas Plant

Uses all types of agricultural raw and residual materials. Provides electricity, gas, and organic fertilizer while reducing dependency on external resources.

Electricity Gas Fertilizer Self-Sufficient
Phase 02

10 Commercial Greenhouses

Modern, technologically efficient greenhouse structures with best-in-class equipment and irrigation systems for intensive year-round agriculture.

Off-Season Crops Climate Control Pest Control Irrigation
Phase 03

Sorting & Packaging

Large fully automatic packaging lines ensuring consistent high quality products through fabrication, cleaning, filling, sealing, labeling, and palletizing.

Automated Quality Control Labeling Export-Ready
Phase 04

Poultry Farm

Two types of poultry: egg-producing hens (2 halls of 40,000) and broiler hens (2 halls of 25,000), providing protein, nutrition, and food security.

80,000 Egg Hens 50,000 Broilers Food Security Nutrition

What We Aim For

Our Objectives

Education & Training

Providing agricultural education and hands-on training to empower community members with sustainable farming skills.

Implementing Green Energy

Building biogas infrastructure to provide clean, renewable energy for the entire farming operation and local community.

Creating New Jobs

Generating meaningful agricultural employment opportunities for the most vulnerable members of the community.

Develop Rural Farming

Transforming barren land into productive agricultural zones using modern farming techniques and technology.

Ending Poverty

Breaking the cycle of poverty through sustainable livelihoods and economic empowerment of rural communities.

Food Security

Ensuring reliable access to nutritious food through diversified farming, poultry production, and modern storage systems.

Global Alignment

UN Sustainable Development Goals

Our work directly contributes to four of the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals, creating measurable global impact.

#7

Affordable & Clean Energy

Our biogas plant provides sustainable energy from agricultural waste, powering operations without fossil fuels.

#9

Modern greenhouses, automated packaging, and smart farming technology driving agricultural innovation.

#10

Empowering genocide survivors and marginalized communities through economic opportunity and dignified work.

#12

Sustainable farming practices, waste-to-energy conversion, and efficient resource management at every phase.
