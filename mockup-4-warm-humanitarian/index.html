About Us

Committed to Growing Rural Communities

RiseMeUp Sustainable Farming is a London-based non-profit charity dedicated to supporting the Nuba Mountain community in Sudan. We work with genocide survivors to build a sustainable, self-sufficient agricultural future.

Our approach transforms underutilized land into thriving agricultural hubs through green energy, modern farming techniques, and community empowerment. We believe that investing in rural people is investing in a brighter future for all.

Our Heart

What Drives Us Forward

Every step we take is guided by our vision for a sustainable future and our mission to empower the most vulnerable communities.

Our Vision

Taking steps towards sustainable development by developing 130 acres of poor-land into horticulture, farming and implementing Green Energy as the main source of infrastructure for the farm. We envision a self-sufficient community powered by innovation and hope.

Our Mission

Turning the 130 acres of poor-land from non-productive land into productive farm land, creating new agricultural job opportunities to help and support the neediest and most excluded within this rural community to end poverty.

Our Projects

Four Phases to Transform Lives

Each phase builds upon the last, creating a comprehensive agricultural ecosystem that drives sustainability and self-sufficiency.

P1

Biogas Plant

Green energy from agricultural materials

Our biogas plant uses all types of agricultural raw and residual materials to produce clean energy. This is the foundation of our farm's infrastructure, providing electricity, gas, and organic fertilizer while reducing dependency on external resources.

Clean Electricity Natural Gas Organic Fertilizer Resource Independence Agricultural Waste Recycling
P2

10 Commercial Greenhouses

Modern, climate-controlled growing facilities

Ten modern, technologically efficient greenhouse structures equipped with the best equipment and irrigation systems. These greenhouses enable year-round production of high-quality crops regardless of external weather conditions.

Off-Season Crops Superior Quality Climate Control Pest Management Efficient Irrigation Year-Round Production
P3

Sorting & Packaging System

Industrial-grade processing and packaging

Large, fully automatic packaging lines ensure consistent, high-quality products ready for market distribution. The complete processing chain covers fabrication, cleaning, filling, sealing, labeling, overwrapping, and palletizing.

Fabrication Cleaning Filling Sealing Labeling Overwrapping Palletizing
P4

Poultry Farm

Protein and nutrition for food security

A comprehensive poultry operation featuring two types of facilities: egg-producing hens housed in 2 halls with capacity for 40,000 birds each, and broiler hens in 2 halls with capacity for 25,000 birds each. This provides critical protein, nutrition, and food security for the community.

80,000 Egg-Laying Hens 50,000 Broiler Hens Protein Production Community Nutrition Food Security

Our Goals

Building a Better Tomorrow

Six core objectives guide every decision we make, from the field to the community and beyond.

Green Energy

Implementing sustainable green energy solutions to power our farming operations independently.

Rural Farming

Developing modern agricultural practices that transform underused land into productive farmland.

Creating Jobs

Generating meaningful employment opportunities for local community members across all farm operations.

Ending Poverty

Breaking the cycle of poverty through agricultural self-sufficiency and economic empowerment.

Education & Training

Providing agricultural education and skills training to empower community members with knowledge.

Food Security

Ensuring stable access to nutritious food for every family in the Nuba Mountain community.
