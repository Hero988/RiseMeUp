Chapter One

The Story

In the heart of Sudan's Nuba Mountains, where the echoes of genocide still linger in the valleys and the scars of conflict mark both land and people, a remarkable transformation is taking root. RiseMeUp Sustainable Farming was born from an unwavering conviction: that the very soil which witnessed unimaginable suffering could become the foundation for renewal, dignity, and lasting hope.

We are a London-based non-profit charity with a singular, ambitious mission — to transform 130 acres of degraded, unproductive land into a thriving agricultural enterprise that serves as both an economic engine and a beacon of possibility for an entire community. Our work is not merely about farming. It is about restoring agency to people who have been systematically excluded, about proving that sustainable development and human dignity are inseparable.

The Nuba Mountain community represents some of the most resilient people on earth. Genocide survivors who refused to let their story end in tragedy. Today, they stand ready to write a new chapter — one defined not by what was taken from them, but by what they are building together. RiseMeUp exists to ensure they have the tools, infrastructure, and support to succeed.

Through a carefully designed four-phase approach — from biogas energy production to commercial greenhouses, from automated packaging to poultry farming — we are constructing a complete, self-sustaining agricultural ecosystem. This is development as it should be: comprehensive, community-driven, and built to endure.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Taking decisive steps towards sustainable development by transforming 130 acres of impoverished land into a flourishing centre of horticulture, farming, and green energy — creating an infrastructure that serves as both a model and a lifeline for the Nuba Mountain community and rural communities worldwide.

Our Mission

Turning 130 acres of non-productive, degraded land into thriving, productive farmland. Creating new agricultural employment opportunities to help, support, and uplift the neediest and most excluded members of this rural community — ultimately working to end the cycle of poverty that has persisted for generations.

The Blueprint

Four Pillars of Transformation

Our comprehensive development plan unfolds in four carefully sequenced phases, each building upon the last to create a self-sustaining agricultural ecosystem.

01

Biogas Plant

The foundation of our energy independence. A decentralised biogas plant that transforms agricultural raw materials and residual waste into clean electricity, gas, and high-nutrient organic fertilizer — dramatically reducing dependency on external resources while strengthening the economic viability of rural farming.

  • Processes all types of agricultural raw and residual materials
  • Generates clean electricity and biogas for the community
  • Produces high-nutrient organic fertilizer from fermentation
  • Reduces dependency on costly external energy sources
  • Decentralised model strengthens local economic value
  • Fully fermented product with reduced odour impact
02

10 Commercial Greenhouses

Modern, technologically advanced greenhouse structures equipped with state-of-the-art irrigation systems for year-round intensive agriculture. These climate-controlled environments enable off-season crop production, superior quality yields, and efficient resource management — while opening entirely new employment opportunities for the community.

  • Ten modern, fully equipped commercial greenhouse structures
  • Advanced irrigation and climate control systems
  • Year-round cultivation including off-season crops
  • Superior crop quality through controlled growing conditions
  • Integrated pest management reduces chemical dependency
  • Creates significant new agricultural employment
03

Sorting & Packaging System

A fully automated, large-scale packaging operation designed to prepare products for both local and international markets. From fabrication and cleaning through filling, sealing, labelling, and palletising — this system ensures consistent, high-quality output that can compete on the global stage.

  • Large fully automatic packaging production lines
  • Complete process: fabrication through palletising
  • Cleaning, filling, sealing, and labelling systems
  • Designed to meet international market standards
  • Ensures consistent, premium-quality product output
  • Overwrapping and palletising for export readiness
04

Poultry Farm

A dual-purpose poultry operation comprising egg-producing layers and broiler production. With capacity for 80,000 laying hens and 50,000 broilers across four purpose-built halls, this phase directly addresses protein deficiency, nutritional security, and food sovereignty for the community.

  • Two halls housing 40,000 egg-producing hens each (80,000 total)
  • Two halls housing 25,000 broiler hens each (50,000 total)
  • Addresses critical protein and nutritional needs
  • Short production cycles for rapid, reliable output
  • Converts agricultural by-products into meat and eggs
  • Strengthens community food security and sovereignty
