Who We Are

Committed to Grow Rural Communities

RiseMeUp Sustainable Farming is a non-profit charity dedicated to supporting the Nuba Mountain community in Sudan, a region that has endured decades of conflict and genocide.

Based in London, we work to transform 130 acres of poor-land into productive farmland, creating sustainable livelihoods through horticulture, farming, and green energy infrastructure.

Our Heart

Vision & Mission

We believe that investing in rural people is investing in a brighter future for all.

Our Vision

Taking steps towards sustainable development by developing 130 acres of poor-land into horticulture, farming and implementing Green Energy as the main source of infrastructure for the farm.

Our Mission

Turning 130 acres of non-productive land into productive farmland, creating new agricultural job opportunities to help, support the neediest and excluded within this rural community to end poverty.

Our Work

Four Phases of Transformation

Each phase builds on the last, creating a self-sustaining ecosystem that transforms lives in the Nuba Mountains.

Phase 1

Biogas Plant

Generating electricity, gas, and fertilizer from agricultural waste. Reducing dependency on external energy sources.

Electricity Gas Fertilizer
Phase 2

10 Commercial Greenhouses

Modern greenhouses with drip irrigation systems. Growing off-season crops with climate and pest control capabilities.

Irrigation Climate Control Off-Season
Phase 3

Sorting & Packaging

Automatic packaging lines for fabrication, cleaning, filling, sealing, labeling, and palletizing of agricultural products.

Automation Quality Export Ready
Phase 4

Poultry Farm

Egg hens (2x40,000) and broilers (2x25,000). Providing protein, nutrition, and food security to the community.

Our Goals

Strategic Objectives

Six core objectives guiding our mission to transform rural communities through sustainable agriculture.

Implementing Green Energy

Building biogas plants and solar infrastructure for sustainable, clean energy powering all farm operations.

Develop Rural Farming

Transforming 130 acres of poor-land into productive farmland through modern agricultural practices.

Creating New Jobs

Generating agricultural employment opportunities for the Nuba Mountain community to build self-sufficiency.

Ending Poverty

Supporting the neediest and most excluded within the rural community through sustainable economic empowerment.

Education & Training

Building capacity through agricultural training programs and educational initiatives for local communities.

Food Security

Ensuring reliable access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food for all community members year-round.
