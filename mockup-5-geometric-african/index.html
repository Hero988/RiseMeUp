Our Heritage

Committed to Grow Rural Communities

RiseMeUp Sustainable Farming is a non-profit charity dedicated to supporting the Nuba Mountain community in Sudan — survivors of genocide rebuilding their lives through agriculture and resilience.

Based in London, we bridge global resources with local needs, channeling investment into sustainable farming infrastructure that empowers communities to achieve food security and economic independence.

Investing in rural people is investing in a brighter future. With nearly 3 billion rural people globally — 40% of the world's population — and agriculture investment being 11 times more effective at reducing poverty, our work is not just meaningful, it is essential.

"Think globally, act locally"
Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Taking steps towards sustainable development by developing 130 acres of poor-land into horticulture, farming and implementing Green Energy as the main source infrastructure of the farm — building a model for rural transformation across Africa.

Our Mission

Turning 130 acres of non-productive land into a productive farm, creating new agricultural job opportunities to help, support the neediest and most excluded within this rural community — and ultimately, to end poverty through sustainable agriculture.

Our Projects

Four Phases of Transformation

Each phase builds upon the last, creating a self-sustaining agricultural ecosystem for the Nuba Mountain community.

Phase 1

Biogas Plant

Our foundation phase establishes a biogas plant that uses all types of agricultural raw and residual materials to produce electricity, gas, and high-nutrient fertilizer — reducing dependency on external energy sources and strengthening rural infrastructure.

Phase 2

10 Commercial Greenhouses

Modern, technologically efficient greenhouse structures equipped with advanced irrigation systems for intensive agriculture. Growing off-season crops with better quality through climate and pest control, maximizing resource efficiency year-round.

Phase 3

Sorting & Packaging System

Large fully automatic packaging lines encompassing fabrication, cleaning, filling, sealing, labeling, overwrapping, and palletizing — designed to make our produce competitive in both local and international markets.

Phase 4

Poultry Farm

A comprehensive poultry operation featuring egg-producing hens (2 halls of 40,000 each) and broiler hens (2 halls of 25,000 each). Providing protein, nutrition, and food security through short, efficient production cycles.

Our Objectives

Six Pillars of Impact

Every project we undertake advances these core objectives for the Nuba Mountain community.

Implementing Green Energy

Building biogas infrastructure to provide clean, renewable energy for the entire farming operation and surrounding community.

Develop Rural Farming

Transforming 130 acres of barren land into productive agricultural zones with modern greenhouses and sustainable methods.

Creating New Jobs

Generating agricultural employment opportunities for the Nuba Mountain community, especially for youth and marginalized groups.

Ending Poverty

Agriculture investment is 11 times more effective at reducing poverty than any other sector. We put that principle into action.

Education & Training

Equipping the next generation with agricultural knowledge and modern farming techniques for long-term sustainability.

Food Security

With poultry, greenhouses, and sustainable farming, we ensure reliable access to nutritious food for thousands of families.
